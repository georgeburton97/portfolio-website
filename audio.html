Audio

Creating audio content is a fascinating process. It’s an invigorating accomplishment to be able to capture a listener’s attention purely through sound. I produce, write, and record all my radio packages and documentaries myself.

Dying to Sleep (Radio Documentary)

George Burton Journalism · Dying To Sleep

In my second year of Uni, I created a radio documentary: Dying to Sleep. This documentary focused on the sleeping disorder, sleep apnoea. As I have a dad who struggles with the condition, this was an interesting topic for me to pursue.

Opening Up (Radio Documentary)

George Burton Journalism · Opening Up

I made this radio documentary to gain an insight into how depression affetcs young people going through education. It involves two sensitive interviews with people who suffered from depression from a young age

Plastic Beach (Radio Package)

George Burton Journalism · Plastic Beach
